Tennessee's Most Invasive Fish Species

Cyprinus carpio

The Common Carp (Cyprinus carpio) is the exotic 8 species first recorded, the widest distribution and the second most abundant (299 records across all basins in 134 years)!

Big Head Carp

Bighead Carp (H. nobilis) shows the fastest expansion 2 rate (Fig. 5), second only to S. trutta, confirming the “highly destructive” and 1 invasive potential of these species (Kolar et al. 2005).
